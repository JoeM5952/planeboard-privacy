Privacy Policy for Planeboard

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Introduction

Planeboard is operated by Joseph Mayeaux. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information when you use Planeboard on your Android device.

Information We Collect

Location Data

Planeboard requests access to your device location to center the live ADS-B map on your current position and to record the location of aircraft you log in your logbook. Location data is used only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Camera

Planeboard requests camera access to photograph aircraft tail numbers and manufacturer data plates for identification purposes. Photos are processed on your device and are not stored or transmitted to our servers.

Logbook Data

Aircraft spotting records you save to your logbook are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data is not transmitted to our servers and is not accessible to us.

Information We Do Not Collect

Planeboard does not collect personal information, does not require account creation, and does not track your usage for advertising purposes. We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third Party Services

Planeboard uses the following third party services to provide aircraft data:

  • AeroDataBox via the Planeboard API for aircraft registration and flight data
  • OpenSky Network for live ADS-B aircraft position data
  • OpenStreetMap and CartoDB for map tiles

These services may collect anonymized request data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data Retention

Logbook data is stored on your device until you delete it. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

Planeboard is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

Since Planeboard does not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You can remove all locally stored logbook data at any time by clearing the app data in your device settings or uninstalling the app.
