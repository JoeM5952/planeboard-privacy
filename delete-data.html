Delete Your Planeboard Data

Planeboard stores all user data locally on your device. We do not store any personal data on our servers. You can delete your data at any time using the steps below.

Delete Data Within the App

  1. Open the Planeboard app
  2. Tap the Logbook tab at the bottom of the screen
  3. Tap Manage in the top right corner
  4. Tap Clear all entries
  5. Confirm deletion when prompted

This permanently deletes all aircraft spotting records from your logbook.

Delete All App Data

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Tap Apps
  3. Find and tap Planeboard
  4. Tap Storage
  5. Tap Clear data
  6. Confirm when prompted

This deletes all locally stored data including your logbook, recent lookups, and app preferences.

What Data Is Deleted

Deleting your data removes:

  • All logbook entries including aircraft registrations, dates, and locations
  • Recent lookup history shown on the home screen
  • App preferences and settings

What Data Is Not Stored

Planeboard does not store any personal data on our servers. We do not retain your location, camera images, or any other personal information. There is no account to delete.
